Food Services

Food is an important part of our daily nourishment. We make sure we keep your child's diet as healthy as possible, because we know how important it is to build strong health habits. The experience we have with many students allows us to provide food that is not only tasty but also nutritious and wholesome.

The nutrition service program is a primary function of the school. It provides healthy, nutritious meals to students at a cost of 500 naira for Junior Secondary Student and 700 naira for Senior Secondary Student

That is why we provide lunch time meals at schools and colleges. A healthy school meal should consist of ten to twelve percent fat, twenty-five to thirty percent protein and fifty percent carbohydrate. Our food service program ensures that students achieve their full potential by ensuring that every meal is nutritious, tasty, and just like home.
