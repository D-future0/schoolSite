School History

The school was founded in 1986 with the motto knowledge is light and was made up of some of the best minds in the industry. Each individual was dedicated and proved to be exceptionally talented. However, it wasn't only these attributes that set us apart from other schools, it was also our ardour for learning, our passion for education and knowledge which simply cannot be matched by either other institutions of learning or simply those who have not had a chance to experience what we have offered them.
