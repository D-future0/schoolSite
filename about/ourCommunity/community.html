Our Community

Our school community is our first priority, and we are committed to providing the resources and education to empower our students. Each student at Creek has a distinct set of needs, and we develop these needs into meaningful opportunities for individual growth through the unique strengths and talents that God has given each one of us. Thanks for visiting Creek International High School, where our hearts are opened through relationships and opportunities that encourage students to live lives full of purpose, passion, and growth.
